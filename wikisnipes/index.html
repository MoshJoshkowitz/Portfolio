WikiSnipes

Made for the 7dfps 2022 game jam.
The challenge was to make an first person shooter game within 1 week.


Working with a friend. We decided to take the classic wikipedia game and turn it into a sniper game. Starting at a random page, every npc is connected to a link(stated above them). When you shoot an npc, the game takes you to its connected link and reshuffles and redistributes new links to the remaining npcs.
While my friend worked on the UI artwork, AI script, and assisted in webscraping wikipedia articles for the links, I worked in the Unity engine to set up the player character, the rest of the programming scripts, and creating the map.
