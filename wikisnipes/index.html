WikiSnipes

Class group project for Video Game Design Studio.
As a class, we were to create a game similar to our Professor's pre-existing space-themed board game.

We decided to create a strategy, resource management, turn-based space game. The players' goal was to either conquer more than half the galaxy with a fleet of space ships or amass enough resources to claim victory.

I was assigned as the lead designer on this project and was in charge of two other designers. While the time constraint of the semester and our skill limitations were issues we ran to early on, the designer's biggest issue we encountered was with the complex and disorganized rule book for the original game we were given by our client. Due to Covid 19, we were unable to playtest the original board game properly, so we only had the rule book to go off of, making figuring out all the rules of the game very challenging. Our solution was to put together our own rule book using the original rule book and our own design changes.
