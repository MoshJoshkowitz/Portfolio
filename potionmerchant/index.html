Potion Merchant

Made for the 2024 Global Game Jam.
The challenge was to make a game within 48 hours with the theme "Make me laugh".

This was my first attempt at making a VR game. In the demo, the player plays as a traveling merchant who sells wacky remedies to their clients. Customres will request what the potion is and what color it's supposed to be. It's up to the player, through trial and error, to craft the requested potion.
Art and sound was done by John823 while I did the rest(design and particle effects, programming).
