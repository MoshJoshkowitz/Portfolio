Strato-Sphere / Chloe

My final project submission for the Intro to Character Design/Development class I took during my junior year at SUNY Polytechnic Institute.

Our task for this project was to design a character and create a game concept that the character would be a part of.

Strato-Sphere is a puzzle game designed for kids in which the player plays as a secret agent and must solve challenging puzzles in order to stop an evil organization's plan and to escape their stronghold.

To assist you is Chloe, a member of the same agency of the player who assists them with some of the puzzles. The catch however is that sometimes the advice and answers she gives are not always correct. Therefore, it is up to the player to use their intuition and problem-solving skills to stop the bad guys and escape the stronghold.

Duration: 3 Weeks
