Sphereport

Sphereport was a game designed by me and two other teammates for our Video Game Design Studio class. Sphereport is a platformer/ puzzle game in which the goal is to reach the goal at the end of the level.

The game's standout mechanic was that the player could throw a teleporting device called a sphereport. When thrown, the player would teleport to the sphereport's location after a few seconds, allowing the player to reach areas not normally possible with the basic character movement before.

My main role on the team was to create and design all the art and assets for Sphereport. I decided to use the website Pixilart.com to create the abandoned testing facility setting and style used in the game.
